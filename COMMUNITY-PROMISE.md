# Community Promise

This module exists to give GMs a free atlas of Golarion. Community feedback
makes it better, and that feedback deserves clear terms. This is our promise
about what we will and will not do with it.

## We will never train AI models on your contributions

Your issues, corrections, lore reports, discussions, pull requests, and any
other feedback given to this project will **never** be used to train,
fine-tune, or otherwise build an AI model. Not by us, and not handed to
anyone else for that purpose.

## What we actually do with feedback

Feedback is used for one thing: fixing and improving the content in this
repository. A report like "this town is in the wrong biome" leads to a
corrected map. Making that correction may involve using existing AI tools
(for example, prompting an image model to regenerate a map with the right
terrain), the same way the original maps were made. Using a tool to fix a
map is not the same as training a model on your words, and the line between
those two is the whole point of this promise.

## No closed value comes out of this repository

Nothing derived from this repository or its community will become a paid
product, a private dataset, or any other closed-source value. What goes in
free stays free, and what comes out is public.

## Free forever

- All code in this repository is and will remain open source under the
  [MIT License](LICENSE).
- All map content operates under
  [Paizo's Community Use Policy](https://paizo.com/licenses/communityuse)
  and carries whatever terms that policy requires. It is fan content, free
  of charge, and not published, endorsed, or specifically approved by Paizo.
- There is no paid tier, no premium version, and no plan for one. If that
  ever changed it would not apply to anything in this repository: everything
  here, and every future release of it, stays free and open under these
  terms.

If you think anything in the project falls short of this promise, open an
issue and say so. Holding us to it is part of the deal.
